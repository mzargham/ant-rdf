## Description

The network of hotel manager, guests, front-desk staff, key, weighted fob, and request sign analyzed by Latour (1991) in 'Technology is society made durable'. The fob translates the moral injunction 'please return your key' into a physical inconvenience that achieves what the polite sign alone cannot.
