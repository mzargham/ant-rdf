## Definition

Moment 3 of translation: definition and coordination of the roles actants will play. Disambiguated from the v1.1 reified ant:EnrolmentRelation (a qualified edge carrying strength/time/contestation) — this class is the Callon process-moment, not the relational state. See R4.

## Source

Callon, M. (1986). Some Elements of a Sociology of Translation.
