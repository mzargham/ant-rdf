# Actant _(Class)_

[← Home](Home)

**IRI:** `https://w3id.org/ant#Actant`

**Label:** Actant

## Definition

Any human or non-human entity participating in a web of relations. Methodological category for the analyst; NOT an ontological commitment about the world. The deliberately pre-categorical term refuses the human/non-human split that material semiotics dissolves (per C2).

## Source

Greimas (semiotics, via Latour); foundational across Callon, Latour, Law.
