# waiverExpires _(Datatype property)_

[← Home](../Home.md)

**IRI:** `https://w3id.org/ant#waiverExpires`

**Label:** waiver expires

## Definition

Optional ISO-date after which the waiver lapses and the warning resurfaces. Most waivers do NOT expire — the default is no expiry (per R9b).
