# ProvInfluencer _(Class)_

[← Home](../Home.md)

**IRI:** `https://w3id.org/ant#ProvInfluencer`

**Label:** PROV Influencer role

## Definition

A role characterizing the target actant *under this perspective* as a prov:Influencer — i.e., as participating in influence relations without a responsibility-bearing claim. Used as ant:assignsRole on a Characterization. Does NOT assert prov:Influencer typing globally. Lighter alternative to ProvAgent for perspectives that refuse pre-network agency attribution.
