**Label:** Constraint waiver

## Definition

An append-only acknowledgement of a Tier-2 SHACL warning, with required justification and waiver-author. Never applies to Tier-1 Violations (which represent structural integrity). Lets analysts honestly override 'should-conform' constraints without silent violation. See §4.6.
