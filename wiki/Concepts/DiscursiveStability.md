**Label:** Discursive stability

## Definition

Stability arising from multi-discursive ordering (Law 1994: managers operating across Enterprise, Bureaucracy, Vocation, Vision; the laboratory needed several modes because each set conditions of possibility for some realities and not others).

## Source

Law, J. (1994). Organizing Modernity; Law, J. (2008).
