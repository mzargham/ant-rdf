# Inscription _(Class)_

[← Home](../Home.md)

**IRI:** `https://w3id.org/ant#Inscription`

**Label:** Inscription

## Definition

A material trace (text, graph, instrument reading, photograph, file) that allows action at a distance and accumulation in centres of calculation. Inscriptions are perspective-agnostic by convention — they are raw materials any perspective may characterize. Candidate alignment: crm:E34_Inscription (see ant-cidoc-align.ttl).
