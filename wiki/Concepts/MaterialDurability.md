**Label:** Material durability

## Definition

Stability arising from durable material delegation — prison walls, knotted bedsheets, sleeping policemen. Bentham's panopticon. 'Stability does not inhere in materials themselves' (Law 2008) — it is the *configuration* of the web that produces durability.

## Source

Law, J. (2008). Actor Network Theory and Material Semiotics.
