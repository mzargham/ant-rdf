**Label:** Mode of ordering

## Definition

A mini-discourse that patterns relations within an organization or assemblage (Law 1994: Enterprise, Bureaucracy, Vocation, Vision). Distinct from Mol's enactment-multiplicity but adjacent. Likely modeled as a SKOS concept scheme in v1.1.

## Source

Law, J. (1994). Organizing Modernity.
