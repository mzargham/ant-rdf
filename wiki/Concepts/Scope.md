## Definition

Act-1 artifact: a declarative selection of what is in analytical view — cases, perspectives, filters. Distinct from ant:Network (an act-4 artifact). A scope can be queried with many different SPARQL patterns; each produces a different view; none of them are *the* network in the scope. See §4.7.
