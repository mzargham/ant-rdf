**Label:** Practice

## Definition

A patterned, situated doing that enacts a reality (Mol; Law). Practices ground perspectives — a perspective is grounded in one or more practices. Used as the value of ant:perPractice on Characterizations to make the observer-frame explicit.

## Source

Mol, A. (2002). The Body Multiple; Law, J. (2008). Actor Network Theory and Material Semiotics.
