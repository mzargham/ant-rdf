# translates _(Object property)_

[← Home](Home)

**IRI:** `https://w3id.org/ant#translates`

**Label:** translates

## Definition

An actant translates another — both 'makes equivalent' (traduction) and 'shifts' (trahison). Loosely, the most generic ANT-relation.

## Source

Serres, M. (1974); Callon, M. (1986); Law, J. (2008).
